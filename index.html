About Rehan School

Founded in 1995, Rehan School has been at the forefront of providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. Our mission is to nurture young minds and prepare them for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that empowers students to achieve their full potential and become responsible global citizens.

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating learning environment with a challenging curriculum that promotes academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning.

School Leadership

Our experienced leadership team brings together decades of educational expertise to guide the school's vision and ensure the highest standards of teaching and learning.

Accreditations

Rehan School is fully accredited by the National Educational Board and has received numerous awards for academic excellence and innovation in teaching.

Academic Programs

At Rehan School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge students and foster a love for learning. Our programs are tailored to meet the needs of students at different stages of their educational journey.

Elementary School

Our elementary program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills through engaging, hands-on learning experiences.

Middle School

The middle school curriculum challenges students with advanced coursework while providing support as they navigate the transition to more independent learning.

High School

Our college-preparatory high school program offers a wide range of Advanced Placement courses and extracurricular opportunities to prepare students for higher education.

Special Programs

We offer specialized programs in STEM, arts, and languages to cater to diverse interests and talents.

Admissions Process

Thank you for your interest in Rehan School. We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn and contribute to our school community.

Application Steps

  1. Submit an online inquiry form
  2. Attend a virtual or in-person tour
  3. Complete the application form
  4. Submit required documents
  5. Schedule a student assessment
  6. Family interview
  7. Receive admission decision

Tuition and Financial Aid

We believe in making quality education accessible. Need-based financial aid is available for qualifying families.

Campus Life

At Rehan School, education extends beyond the classroom. We offer a vibrant campus life with numerous activities, clubs, and sports programs.

Student Activities

From robotics club to debate team, students have opportunities to explore their interests and develop new skills.

Athletics

Our sports programs promote teamwork, discipline, and healthy competition. We offer soccer, basketball, swimming, track and field, and more.

Arts Programs

Community Service
