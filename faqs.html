FAQs about Rehan School Korangi Campus

What is the Rehan School Korangi campus?

Rehan School Korangi campus is an educational institution in Karachi, Pakistan, founded by Rehan Allahwala. It focuses on providing a modern and comprehensive education that integrates technology and soft skills training to prepare students for future challenges.

Where is the Rehan School Korangi campus located?

The campus is located in Sector 50 C, Korangi, Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan.

What is the mission of Rehan School?

The mission of Rehan School is to empower students with the skills necessary to earn a substantial income and to become problem solvers. The school aims to alleviate poverty by providing students with practical and technological education.

What makes Rehan School unique?

Rehan School is the first AI-enabled school in Pakistan. It emphasizes teaching students soft skills and technological skills that allow them to start earning while they are still studying. This approach is designed to create future leaders and innovators who can contribute to solving societal challenges.

What facilities are available at the Rehan School Korangi campus?

The campus features modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, sports facilities, and other amenities designed to provide a holistic educational experience.

How does Rehan School integrate technology into its curriculum?

Rehan School integrates technology by introducing students to advanced technological tools and learning methods. This includes AI tools and other digital resources that help students stay ahead in the rapidly evolving digital world.

What is the primary goal of the curriculum at Rehan School?

The primary goal of the curriculum is to prepare students not only academically but also practically. This includes developing marketable skills that allow students to earn an income while still in school, fostering financial independence and entrepreneurial spirit.

Who founded Rehan School and why?

Rehan School was founded by Rehan Allahwala. His vision is to end poverty in Pakistan by equipping students with the knowledge and skills needed to solve problems and seize opportunities. He believes that education is the key to empowering individuals and transforming society.

Are there virtual tours available for the Rehan School Korangi campus?

Yes, virtual tours are available. These tours provide an immersive view of the campus facilities, including classrooms, labs, and sports areas. The virtual tour can be viewed on our tour page and our official YouTube channel.

How can I learn more about Rehan School?

For more detailed information, you can visit the official Rehan School website or explore their video content on YouTube, which provides insights into the school’s facilities, mission, and educational approach.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to educate and mentor the next generation of innovative leaders within 10 years, under the direct supervision of Rehan Allahwala. We aim to equip our students with the academic foundation and real-world skills to become not only future Nobel laureate contenders but also successful entrepreneurs who can tackle the world’s most pressing issues.
