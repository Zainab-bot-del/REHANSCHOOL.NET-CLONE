About Us

Rehan School – Making Problem Solvers of Tomorrow

Step inside the Rehan School Korangi campus in Karachi, Pakistan, and experience a journey of innovation and empowerment. Explore the school’s modern facilities, pioneering educational methods, and inspiring atmosphere that fosters creativity and entrepreneurship in students.

At Rehan School, we embrace a transformative approach to education with our motto: “No Pen, No Book.” In our innovative learning environment, students harness the power of technology, utilizing laptops and smartphones as their primary tools for education.

At Rehan School, we align with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), integrating TEDx videos to spark critical thinking. Our students actively shape their learning journey, creating educational videos that they share on social media. This hands-on approach not only deepens understanding but also enriches the broader educational dialogue. Join us in fostering creativity, innovation, and meaningful impact!

Wala & Wali Concepts

At Rehan School, Rehan Allahwala has introduced the “wala” and “wali” concepts. Students align their names with their assigned Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), replacing their last names accordingly. This unique practice personalizes their connection to specific SDGs and fosters a sense of commitment to addressing societal and environmental challenges through their chosen professions.

Cutting-Edge Learning Spaces

Explore creativity in our podcast rooms—introducing Studio Diamond and Studio Platinum. Students amplify their voices, conduct insightful interviews, and build confidence through engaging podcast sessions in these state-of-the-art facilities.

UNGA Replica for Real-World Learning

Rehan School’s Korangi campus proudly unveils a unique learning space—a meticulously replicated United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) room. This environment empowers students to delve into real-world conflict resolution scenarios using social media. Students engage with global peers, cultivating a profound understanding of diverse perspectives. The UNGA replica serves as a dynamic hub for meaningful global discourse.

Soft Skills & Practical Knowledge

At the Rehan School Korangi campus, students develop essential soft skills crucial for professional development. The curriculum goes beyond traditional subjects, incorporating skills like graphic design, video editing, coding, and Canva proficiency. Students also gain practical knowledge on platforms such as Fiverr, Upwork, and YouTube, preparing them for freelancing and entrepreneurial opportunities globally.

This holistic approach ensures graduates possess both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, ready for success in today’s professional landscape.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to educate and mentor the next generation of innovative leaders within 10 years, under the direct supervision of Rehan Allahwala. We aim to equip our students with the academic foundation and real-world skills to become not only future Nobel laureate contenders but also successful entrepreneurs who can tackle the world’s most pressing issues.
