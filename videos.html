Rehan Allahwala Explains the Pattern of Class VII of Rehan School

In this video, Rehan Allahwala explains the pattern of class VII in the Rehan School Korangi campus. As the founder of Rehan School, Rehan Allahwala’s mission is that every single student can earn at least 100,000 a month by learning soft skills. The pattern is specially designed for class VII students to learn soft skills and start making money while studying.

What Is The Purpose Behind Making Rehan School? Rehan Allahwala Explains

In this video, Rehan Allahwala explains the purpose of making the Rehan School Korangi campus. His mission is to end poverty in Pakistan. He explains that poverty can be eliminated if we understand the challenges and learn how to solve them. Kids at Rehan School are trained to become future problem-solvers, learning technology, solution-finding, and skills from those who overcame similar challenges. Once graduated, these students will possess “superpowers” to find solutions effectively.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to educate and mentor the next generation of innovative leaders within 10 years, under the direct supervision of Rehan Allahwala. We aim to equip our students with the academic foundation and real-world skills to become not only future Nobel laureate contenders but also successful entrepreneurs who can tackle the world’s most pressing issues.
