Launch Your Physical School

Starting your own Rehan School on-site is a transformative journey. We provide a step-by-step guide, including selecting the right location, setting up the infrastructure, laptop and mobile vendors, equipment related to podcasts, internet connection, network, and hiring qualified staff. Our detailed manual covers everything from initial planning to execution, ensuring you have the resources and knowledge to create a successful educational institution.

Launch Your Remote School

Starting a remote Rehan School allows you to reach students beyond geographical boundaries. Our guide includes selecting the right digital platforms, setting up virtual classrooms, and training teachers for remote instruction. We also provide best practices for maintaining engagement and delivering high-quality education online.

Engaging Summer Camps

Our summer camps offer a variety of activities designed to enhance learning and provide fun, enriching experiences. From tech camps to creative arts, our programs help students explore new interests and develop new skills in a supportive environment.

“Digital Mastery for a Connected World,” is Rehan School’s upcoming summer camp where learning meets fun! In this engaging camp, you’ll dive into AI with ChatGPT, create awesome videos, design stunning graphics, and make your own YouTube videos. The camp runs for three hours a day, five days a week, offering an immersive experience that blends creativity with technology. Requirements include permission to use social media, a laptop with 4GB of memory, and a mobile phone with 4GB of RAM. Get ready to enhance your digital skills and unleash your creativity!

Laptop Vendors

To ensure students have access to the best technology, we have OLPP (One Laptop Per Pakistani) in place. The OLPP offers competitive prices and reliable products, suitable for both on-site and remote learning environments.

Mobile Vendors
